Nachos

Ingredients

  • 175g packet plain tortilla chips
  • 225g jar salsa
  • a few sliced spring onions
  • a good chunk of cheese
  • a pinch of crushed chilli flakes
  • sour cream or guacamole (optional)

Instructions

  1. Tip tortilla chips on to a baking tray, pour over salsa and scatter over a few sliced spring onions
  2. Grate over a good chunk of cheese (fontina is perfect, but cheddar will do).
  3. Add a pinch of crushed chilli flakes, then put under a hot grill for about 3 minutes to melt the cheese.
  4. Serve with soured cream or guacamole (optional)
