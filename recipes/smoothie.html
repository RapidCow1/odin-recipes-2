Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 orange, peeled and quartered
  • 1/3 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup water or milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1 to 2 teaspoons honey, optional

Instructions

  1. Roughly chop the banana and orange quarters, and then add to a blender.
  2. Top with yogurt and water (or milk). Turn blender on and blend until creamy and smooth.
  3. Taste, and then adjust with honey if needed.
